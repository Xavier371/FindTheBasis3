Xavier Math Games

Catch the Point

An exciting strategic pursuit game where players navigate through a dynamic grid system. Play in offense mode to catch the red point, or switch to defense mode to evade capture. The challenge intensifies as paths disappear after each move, requiring quick thinking and careful planning. Features both single-player AI and two-player modes, making it perfect for testing your tactical skills alone or competing with friends.

Play Catch the Point

Find the Basis

A unique linear algebra visualization game that challenges your understanding of vector transformations. Manipulate basis vectors (i' and j') to transform the coordinate space and map a blue point onto a red target. Through rotations, reflections, and scaling, discover how 2x2 matrices transform space. Perfect for students learning linear transformations or anyone interested in interactive mathematical visualization.

Play Find the Basis

Graph the Match

An engaging optimization puzzle based on the maximum weighted matching problem. Connect nodes between two sets to create the highest-scoring matching, where no vertex can be used twice. Inspired by real-world applications like job matching and ride-sharing algorithms, this game combines graph theory with practical problem-solving. Adjust the size of each set to increase the challenge and test your optimization skills.

Play Graph the Match
