Xavier Math Games

Catch the Point

The game begins with you as the blue point, trying to catch the red point. Every move, a random edge is removed from the game, try to corner the red point before an edge is removed and the points are separated. Switch the defense mode to try to evade the red point for as long as possible, and switch to two players to play with a friend.

Find the Basis

The vectors i and j form the unit square that spans all 2 dimensional space. Drag and drop i' and j' to transform the unit square so that the blue point falls on red. Once you believe your transformation will correctly map the initial blue vector onto the red, press go and try to get it as fast as possible. Click solve to see how you get to a (non unique) solution. Click help to learn more about how the game works.

Graph the Match

The red points are you and your friends, the blue points are another friend group. Each person can talk to exactly one person at a time, and the edge weights represent the compatibility score between those two people. Try and click the set of edges that maximize the total compatibility score for the matching.
